About Us

RailConnect is an all-in-one platform designed to simplify and enhance the Indian railway travel experience by unifying essential services such as local and express ticket booking, live train running status, PNR checks, food ordering, and station facility information into a single user-friendly application. Our mission is to eliminate the need for multiple apps and streamline the journey for commuters, tourists, and business travelers alike. With features powered by AI for personalized travel suggestions and smart notifications, RailConnect aims to modernize railway travel, improve convenience, and reduce stress, ultimately contributing to the digital transformation of Indian Railways.
